Paraquat dichloride is a chemical compound that has been present in many herbicides since the 1950s. Those who work with this herbicide must be trained in the correct handling of it and certified for its use.

Paraquat has been linked with a high incidence of Parkinson’s Disease in those who have handled the chemical. Despite proper care and training in the use of this chemical compound, many people who have been exposed to it have suffered negative health effects. If you have worked with Paraquat in the past and have developed Parkinson’s, you might be able to seek compensation..

What Health Conditions Is Paraquat Exposure Linked to?


Parkinson’s disease is the main condition that is linked to Paraquat exposure. However, there are other potential health conditions that could be considered related to exposure to this chemical. Syngenta and Chevron are the two main companies that sell products containing this chemical compound and are the defendants of the largest proportion of the class action lawsuits that are currently active.

The number of pending cases for those who have developed health problems after exposure to Paraquat now numbers at almost 5,000. While the manufacturers contend that the product cannot be linked with poor health outcomes in people who have been exposed to it, there are many studies that demonstrate a direct link between Parkinson’s and Paraquat.

There are other health conditions and qualifying symptoms that have been linked to exposure to Paraquat in studies. Some of these conditions/symptoms include but are not limited to: (In order to qualify for these additional health conditions/symptoms, you must actively be taking Parkinson's Disease medications)



  • Tremors (hand or other)
  • Prolonged or Extreme Stiffness
  • Difficulty With Body Movements
  • Loss Of Balance
  • Difficulty or Soft Speaking
  • Reduced Facial Expressions (blank stare)
  • Drooling
  • Small Handwriting
  • Trembling
  • Whole Body Fatigue or Dizziness
  • Amnesia or Confusion In Evenings
  • Early Awakenings or Sleep Disturbances
  • Anxiety or Apathy
  • Hallucinations
  • Delusions
